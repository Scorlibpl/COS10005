ABOUT BRUNO'S

WHAT WE ARE

Welcome to Brunos Pizzeria! Our menu has includes all your favorite pizzas and so much more! We are known as a leader in the delivery service business accompanied by a very significant growth on takeaway service. Brunos pizzeria ranks among the world's top public restaurant brands with more than 1.000 outlets in more than 5 countries. Our motto is to sell more pizza and have more fun.

We're right in Hawthorn and enjoy serving you either inside our restaurant, with takeaway, or from the comfort of your own home. Our handtossed products that are known in the world are made manually by hand instead of machine. All the products we present are made from the highest quality products and raw materials. We also provide a 30-minute warranty guarantee to deliver hot pizza to your order.
