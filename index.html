Ranking and ratings

Swinburne has an international reputation for work on quality that connects science and technology with industry and society. Swinburne is ranked among the worlds top 400 universities by QS World University Rankings
and Top 200 for Engineering and Technology by the World University Rankings 2020 by subject. Swinburne position in prestigious world academic ranking lists represents dedication to high-quality teaching and study results for graduates.

Campuses and maps

Swinburne have three Melbourne-based campuses in Hawthorn, Croydon and Wantirna and one in Sarawak, Malaysia. The campuses are located close to public transit an offer creative learning opportunities to help students in their studies.

Student will also have access to clubs and societies, community facilities and great places to enjoy coffee and socialise with friends.
